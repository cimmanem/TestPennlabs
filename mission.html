Our Mission

We hope to create a community of innovators dedicated to building solutions for the most pressing problems in healthcare. We believe that access to a clinical data toolkit and our experienced mentors can help to incubate great ideas into reality, when matched with the investment, creativity, and excitement of individuals within the Penn community.
