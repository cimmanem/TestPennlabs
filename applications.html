What makes a great team?

While we will consider all teams on an ongoing basis, we encourage teams to include individuals with the following roles:

Problem Sponsor

(Physician, Nurse)
Experts who have identified a pain point and/or intervention on the wards. They are potential end users of the solution, and future clinical champions of the product.

Supervisor

(Resident, Engineering instructor)
Mentors who will advise project teams on a weekly basis. They are skilled in IT, data analytics, and/or coding. They will be a resource as-needed to help shape the team's progress and developement.

Designer

(Medical student, Design student)
Individuals who have first-hand experience in the problems and end-users of a potential product. They have the language to communicate the needs and workflow of the clinical user.

Developer

(Engineering student)
Individuals who are able to build webapps and have established experience with coding, and who have the desire to learn more. This project will fulfill credit through the Joint Engineering/Medicine Ventures Class.

What next?

Get in touch with us below, and we will meet with you to begin the conversation! For the most promising teams, we will grant access to the program's toolkit, including space on an internal server and the clinical data API.
